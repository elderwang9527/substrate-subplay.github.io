Play/Subplay development plan vote

At present, the Play test network has successfully passed the test. There are no major issues, and some minor issues will be fixed once the Play network is officially launched. Play start-up plan: ABCD has been basically completed, only left the step to launch the official main network. But Play won’t take off if it just launches the mainnet and doesn’t do anything afterwards. So I set up this plan, and holders could either send token to 5E1sjYVKXosAWXutpj5qiCi3ZwEFBvAdqwJrCSn36F6GEX31 on the new test network to say yes to this plan, or send token to 5CtcUo6BYp7UXK8wfBgv1EoxpowdYabofjA5Y1vwmgbm9ybZ to say no to this plan. I will count the balance of the two addresses at 24:00 on January 21th. If the balance of the former is greater than the latter, I will start the main network according to this plan and carry out future work according to this plan. If not, I will still start the main network and the primary token distribution will be exactly the same as the beta test network, but I will no longer volunteer for this work, just like a normal owner, I will expect someone else to promote or develop Play. The plan can be broadly divided into the following three parts:

1, Name the official mainnet Subplay (token name still uses PLAY)

The name Play is easy to remember, but it’s a common word that feels too casual and not serious enough, and it’s also makes it hard to promote, if anyone is interested in the chain, search for the word. The Play chain is likely to be overwhelmed by other information. Therefore, I plan to name it Subplay, because this chain, like polkadot and other chains, is based on Substrate technology, so the two are merged and called Subplay. On the one hand, it respects the opinions of AP, and on the other hand, it improves the recognition of the chain. At the same time, sub has the meaning of underground, so subplay can also be understood as subPlayGround or subPlayStation, giving a cool cyberpunk feel, which is also in line with its initial intended use as the substrate developer to practice and test.

2, Official mainnet will add 200 million Play tokens as a development fund for the development, publicity and promotion of Subplay in the future. Including but not limited to

(1) Create and maintain a history node, develop a blockchain browser or apply for an existing blockchain browser, so that users can easily check the detailed history of their account;

(2) Reward the validators. Due to the low price of Play currency at the initial stage, the income obtained by the verifier according to the default setting of the system cannot cover the server cost of the validators. Therefore, additional rewards are required at the initial stage to attract enough validators to participate.

(3) Airdrops to target people such as substrate developers to attract more people to join.

(4) Invite other good Substrate projects to deploy to the Subplay chain.Increases the vitality of Subplay.

(5) Apply for PLAY to put on wallet, exchange and so on.

(6) award any team or individual who contributes to Subplay.For example, developers who create applications on Subplay, volunteers who translate Chinese information such as using tutorials into English, ordinary users or KOLs who are eager to promote Subplay on social media, etc.

3, I will work full time for Subplay throughout 2021, but in return I will receive 167.88 million Play tokens as an incentive, so the initial total amount of Play will be 1.3 billion. But I promise that the 167.88 million token that reward me will not be sold through the whole 2021. I will do the following work including but not limited to:

(1) Create and maintain Subplay official GitHub, official website, etc. introduce Subplay to the outside world, and regularly publish Subplay project plan and progress.

(2) In the subplay WeChat group, the telegram group answers all kinds of questions;

(3) Learn Substrate over time and create a DApp to deploy to Subplay, or clone and deploy open source DApps to Subplay.

(4) Communicate and persuade as many people as possible to deploy the validators to increase the decentralization and security of Subplay.

(5) Create text, pictures, and video tutorials. Let users understand various functions, familiar with various operations.

(6) Optimize the use experience of Subplay. For example, Chinese users often fail to connect or the connection is too slow when the Play test website is connected with polkadot official browser. After the launch of Subplay mainnet, it will consider to deploy functions similar to polkadot official browser, but the server will be deployed in China, so that Chinese users can connect more quickly.

(7) Liaise and communicate with Substrate community to introduce Subplay. At least let developers know there is a Subplay chain, giving them one more option when developing projects. In addition to the usual introduction in the network, will also be introduced in reality. Like there will be a Substrate Developers Meeting in Chengdu on 30th of this month, if the plan can be approved, I will attend the meeting and introduce the Subplay project to the developers.

(8) In the early stage, I will create a subplay OTC trading group and act as a trading guarantor, so that PLAY holders can trade PLAY safely when PLAY didn’t listed in any exchange.

(9) When parallel threading technology is mature, let Subplay connect to polkadot via parallel threads. If it can grow in the future, it can also consider buying a polkadot slot to become a poladot parallel chain and share the polkadot ecology.

(10) I will be involved in all the work arising from the use of the project funds, as mentioned in the second point. In addition, I will set up a dedicated telegram channel for the publication of every use of the fund, so that the use of the fund is transparent and open.

I expect that holders will have many questions about this plan, so I will list and answer some of them here:

1, Why didn’t pubilc the plan before mapping the PLAY token?

My initial idea was that all tokens were completely mapped and no additional tokens were issued. As for the rewards for those who contribute to the project, they can be obtained from Subplay’s Treasury function. However, during the actual test, I realized that due to the currency price of PLAY, the value generated by Treasury may only be worth more than 100 yuan per month, which is not enough to provide incentives at all. But the mapping is already underway, so I’ll have to wait until now to announce the new plan. But I did not break my promise, because in the mapping plan, I only promised to open the PLAY chain, I did not promise that I would still work for the chain for free after the official main network was opened.

2, Who controls the project fund?

Although I hope with a democratic way, control the fund, but according to my participation in other project experience, this way for a small project will bring a lot of problems, the most serious problem is holders have different oppinions about how to use the fund, such as short-term holders want to cash as soon as possible, they want a quick pump and dump game, so that they can get money quickly. The long-term holders will want to build as much infrastructure as possible so that the chain is really of high value, and then make reasonable money as it grows. These discordant ideas will lead to persistent quarrels, serious internal friction, impact on the relationship between holders, reduce the efficiency of the use of funds, and ultimately lead to project failure. So in this plan, the fund will be under my control, but I will disclose every use of the fund. For larger fund expenditures, I would ask the community, and if the majority strongly opposed the expenditures, I would not force them to pass. If Subplay grows in the future, we can also set up an institution like polkadot Council to make the use of the fund more democratic.

3, Will I take kickbacks using project funds?

First of all, every expenditure of the Fund is transparent and open, and subject to oversight at all times. In addition, those of you who have worked on previous ENU projects should know that I am very averse to kickbacks. At that time, some members of the ENU community were planning to spend more than 5 million yuan in funds to get ENU to list at a less popular exchange. Many members of the community oppose the spending, which they see as kickbacks. I was also strongly opposed to this proposal and was therefore hated by many so-called community KOLs. So if I took a kickback from the project fund, I would be humiliate myself. In addition, if I use these funds to take rebates, it is bound to make Subplay grow less well than expected, then the value of 167.88 million PLAY will be greatly reduced in 2022, so I will not take rebates no matter to protect my reputation or interests.

4, Is this a plan to get something for nothing?

I pay real money for my Play, just like any other Play holder. I can be announce, the adderss that has the second largest number of PLAY in the mapping: 5Hg1A6igTPS3t4nLoN4SRTjtjEWU8QThNnUEzzkHXUGnRAnK is owned by be. In my plan, the coins in this account will not be sold throughout 2021, except for the possibility that they will be transferred to a more secure cold wallet address. (It is my plan to lock these self-mapped tokens, which may change in case of urgent like need for money. But locking up the tokens that reward me is my promise, so these tokens that reward me are not going to be sold in 2021 anyway). So I really spent a lot of my money to invest in this project, and I hope to build Subplay for a long time, and then benefit from it. But not try to get something for nothing from this plan.

5, Why work full-time for Subplay in only 2021?

After a year of working on all aspects of Subplay, it’s easy to see where Subplay can go. If after a year of work there is no improvement, then we can see maybe the chain really does not have much hope and I need to find another route. Of course, the best outcome is that after a year of hard work, Subplay can grow, then we can renegotiate the new plan, I can still continue to work for Subplay even for free.

6, Why promise not to sell reward tokens for the entire year of 2021?

The idea is to tell investors that I want to build the Subplay chain over the long term, to make it of real value, and then make money in a reasonable way. Rather than like many other projects to pull new then cut leek to get cheat money. Later, if technology allows, I will use smart contracts to make the 167.88 million reward tokens immobile for the entire year of 2021. Although this project is not a scam project, I still hope everyone invest rationally. Just because it is not a scam project does not mean that you will definitely make money by investing in it, just as it does if you invest in Bitcoin.

7, Am I the Subplay project owner?

First of all, I am not the initiator of the project. It was first proposed by AP. I just took the lead in starting the chain. I won’t execute the project until half of the Play Coins vote to approve it, so I’m not the project owner. You can think of Subplay as a small factory owned by the collective, with all the factory owners voting for me to be the director and sole worker. At the same time, I am also a shareholder in the factory, so I will do everything possible to make the factory well out of both my responsibility and my own interests. So later on, if all goes well, the factory will expand and more workers will be able to come in. If it develops better, a board of directors can be set up in the future to make the operation of the factory more standardized and democratic.

Play/Subplay发展计划投票

目前PLAY测试网已经成功通过测试。没有遇到大问题，有些小问题也会在PLAY网络正式上线后修正。PLAY启动计划: https://www.weibo.com/ttarticle/p/show?id=2309404590818374582447 已基本完成，只差正式上线主网了。但如果仅是上线主网，之后什么都不做，PLAY肯定很难发展起来。所以我拟定了这个计划，各位持有者可以在新开的测试网中向 5E1sjYVKXosAWXutpj5qiCi3ZwEFBvAdqwJrCSn36F6GEX31 转账，表示支持此计划，或者向 5CtcUo6BYp7UXK8wfBgv1EoxpowdYabofjA5Y1vwmgbm9ybZ 转账，表示反对此计划。我会在1月21日24：00统计两个地址余额情况，如果前者大于后者我会按此计划启动主网并进行以后的工作。如相反，则我仍会启动主网，主代币分布将完全和测试网相同，但我也不会再自愿做相关工作，只会像普通持有者一样期待有其他人推广或开发PLAY。计划大致可分为以下三点：

一，将正式主网命名为Subplay（代币名称仍然使用PLAY）：

Play这个名字虽然常见，好记。但它是一个常用词，一方面让人感觉太随意。另一方面也让它很难推广，如果有人对这个链感兴趣，搜索这个词。Play链很可能会被其它信息淹没。所以我计划给他命名为subplay，因为这条链和波卡等链一样，都是基于substrate技术，所以两者融合而称之为subplay。一方面尊重了AP的意见，一方面也提高了这条链的辨识度。同时，sub有地下的意思，所以subplay也可以理解为subPlayGround（地下游乐场）或subPlayStation（地下游戏站），给人一种酷酷的赛博朋克的感觉，也符合它初期作为给substrate开发者练手，测试的预期用途。

二，正式主网将新增2亿PLAY代币作为发展基金，用于今后对subplay的开发，宣传，推广等工作。包括但不限于：

1，创建并维护历史节点，开发区块链浏览器或申请上架已有的区块浏览器，从而让用户能轻易查询账户的详细历史记录；

2，奖励验证人节点，由于初期PLAY币价很低，按系统的默认设置验证人获得的收益无法覆盖验证人服务器花费，所以初期需要有额外的奖励，才能吸引足够的验证人参与。

3，对如substrate开发者等目标人群进行针对性空投，吸引更多人加入。

4，邀请各种substrate项目部署到subplay链上。增加subplay活力。

5，申请PLAY上架各种数字货币钱包，交易所。

6，激励任何为subplay做贡献的团队或个人。如在subplay上创建应用的开发者，翻译中文信息如使用教程等为英文的志愿者，热心在社交媒体上推广subplay的普通用户或KOL等。

三，我将在2021年全年为subplay全职工作，但作为回报，我将获得1.6788亿PLAY代币作为奖励，这样PLAY初始总量将为13亿。我承诺这些奖励代币在整个2021年都不会卖出。我将做包括但不限于以下工作：

1，创建并维护subplay官方github，官方网站等，向外界介绍subplay，并定期公布subplay项目计划和进展；

2，在subplay微信群，电报群解答各类疑问；

3，长期学习substrate，并创建dapp部署到subplay上，或克隆并部署开源的dapp到subplay上。

4，沟通并劝说尽量多的人部署验证人节点，增加subplay的去中心化和安全性。

5，创建各类文字，图片，视频教程。让用户搞懂各种功能，熟悉各种操作。

6，优化subplay使用体验，如目前play测试网使用波卡官方浏览器连接，中国用户经常出现连接不上或连接太慢的问题。subplay主网上线后，将考虑部署功能和波卡官方浏览器类似，但服务器部署在中国的浏览器，方便中国用户更快速连接。

7，与substrate开发者社区保持联系并沟通，介绍subplay。至少让开发者们知道有subplay这一条链，让他们开发项目时能多一个选项。除平常在网络中介绍外，也会在现实中介绍。如本月30日在成都会有一个substrate开发者聚会，如此项计划能通过，我将参与此聚会，并向开发者们介绍subplay项目。

8，前期会创建一个subplay场外交易群，并做交易担保人，让未上任何交易所时，PLAY持有者也可安全的交易PLAY。

9，当平行线程技术成熟后，让subplay通过平行线程连接上波卡。以后若能发展壮大，也可考虑购买波卡卡槽，成为一条波卡平行链，分享波卡生态。

10，项目基金的使用所产生的各项工作，即第二点所说项目基金工作我都将参与。此外，我还会建立一个专用的电报频道，用于公布基金的每笔用途，使基金的使用透明公开。

估计各位持有者会对这个计划有很多疑问，所以我在这里把可能出现的疑问列举并解答下：

1，为什么不在映射开始时就公布这个计划？

我最初的想法是所有代币完全按映射发放，不额外发放任何代币。至于对项目做贡献的人的奖励，可以从subplay的treasury（目前一般翻译为国库）功能里获得奖励。但我实际测试时才意识到，由于PLAY的币价，treasury所产生的价值可能每月才值一百多元，根本不足以提供激励。但此时映射已经在进行中，我就只好等到现在才来宣布这个新计划。但我也没有违背我的承诺，因为在映射计划中，我只是承诺会开启PLAY链，并未承诺主网开启后仍会无偿为这条链工作。

2，项目基金由谁控制？

虽然我希望用民主的方式控制这笔基金，但根据我参与其它项目的经验，这种方式对小项目会带来很多严重的问题，最严重的问题就是持币人对基金使用意见不一，比如短期持有者想尽快变现，就会希望像山寨空气币那样尽快拉新，然后割韭菜（我觉得这就是ENU死掉和YAS最终发展不好的原因之一）。而长线持有者会希望尽量建好基础设施，让这条链真的具备比较高的价值，然后在发展壮大后合情合理的赚到钱。这些不一致的想法会导致大家持久的争吵，造成严重的内耗，影响持有者之间的关系，降低基金使用效率，最终导致项目失败。所以这个计划里基金将由我控制，但我会公开每一次基金的使用。对于大额的基金开支，我会询问社区意见，如果大多数人强烈反对该项开支，我也不会强制通过。如果subplay以后能发展壮大，我们也可以设立像波卡理事会那样的机构，让基金使用更加民主。

3，我会不会利用项目基金吃回扣？

首先，基金的每项开支都透明公开，接受大家随时监督。此外，参与过以前ENU项目的人应该知道，我是非常反感吃回扣的。当时ENU社区一些人计划花费五百多万人民币的基金让ENU上线一个并不火的交易所。AP和很多国外社区成员都反对这项开销，认为存在吃回扣的嫌疑。我也强烈反对这项提议，因此被很多所谓的社区KOL带节奏黑。所以如果我拿项目基金吃回扣的话，就是在自己打自己脸。此外，如果我用这些基金吃回扣，也必然让subplay发展不如预期，那么我在2022年可以使用的奖励代币的价值也会大打折扣。所以不管是考虑到自己的声誉或利益，我都不会吃回扣。

4，这个计划是不是想空手套白狼？

我跟其它PLAY持有者一样，都是花了真金白银购买PLAY的。我可以公布，映射PLAY第二多的账号：5Hg1A6igTPS3t4nLoN4SRTjtjEWU8QThNnUEzzkHXUGnRAnK 就是我所持有的。在我的计划里，这个账号里的币除了可能会转到一个更安全的冷钱包地址外，在整个2021年不会卖出一个（锁住这些我自己映射的代币是我的计划，也许会遇到如急需用钱等情况而改变计划。但锁住奖励我的代币则是我的承诺，所以这些奖励我的代币无论如何我是不会在2021年卖掉的）。所以我是花了很多资金投金投资这个项目，即使不是投资最多的也是最多的前几人。并且我是希望长期建设subplay，然后获益。并不是想空手套白狼占各位持有者便宜。

5，为什么只在2021年为subplay全职工作？

因为经过一年的各方面工作，subplay能发展到什么程度就已经可以预见。如果经过一年的工作也不见起色，那就说明这条链确实没有太大希望，我就需要另寻他路了。当然，最好的结果是经过一年的努力，subplay能发展起来，那到时候大家又可以重新商议新计划，我仍可以继续甚至免费为subplay工作。

6，为什么承诺在整个2021年不卖出奖励代币？

这样做是想告诉投资者，我希望长期建设subplay链，让它具有真实的价值，然后再合情合理的赚钱。而不是像很多其它项目那样搞拉新割韭菜骗钱。后续如果技术允许，我会用智能合约让这1.6788亿奖励代币在整个2021年无法移动。虽然这个项目不是骗钱项目，但我还是希望大家理性投资，不是骗钱的项目不代表你就一定会因为投资它赚钱，就像你投资了比特币也不代表你一定会赚钱一样。

7，我是subplay的项目方吗？

首先我不是项目发起人，最先是由AP倡议，我只是带头开启这个链。而只有当半数的PLAY币投票认可这个计划，我才会按照计划执行这个项目，所以我也不是项目方。可以把subplay想象为一个集体所有的小工厂，所有工厂持股人投票选出我当厂长兼唯一的工人。而我自己也是工厂的一个持股人，一方面出于责任，一方面出于自己的利益，我肯定会想方设法把工厂搞好。这样以后如果一切发展顺利，工厂将扩大，就能招收更多的工人进来。如果再发展得更好，将来还可以成立董事会，让这个工厂的运营更规范，民主。
